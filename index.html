Hello, My Name is Zakir.

36 years young, husband and father, coffee addict. Highly motivated creative developer with international client portfolio.

I'm a full stack WordPress Developer (Yes both front and backend).
I mainly work in two ways:

  1. With web designers and agencies: I build the sites they design, from PSDs to completed site.
  2. With individual small businesses and solopreneurs: I act as a 'web fairy', supporting them in their online lives.

In all aspects of my work, I aim to do the following things:

  • Make every site I build as easy to use as possible, both for visitors and for the site's managers. WordPress is the world's best loved CMS because its the world's most user-friendly CMS, and features such as custom post types and taxonomies allow for a truly intuitive admin area. I take pride in finding creative ways to make custom features simple and intuitive for the site's owner to manage.
  • Write good, clean, semantic code which is good for both humans and search engines.
  • Make sure that every client can use the site I've built for them. I love teaching people to use WordPress, and often create training videos to help them going forwards.
  • Be reliable, supportive, and easy to work with. My job doesn't stop with the coding: I believe in creating a true partnership with my clients. It's a matter of immense pride to me that they consistently feed back that they appreciate not only *what* I do, but *how* I do it.
  • Conduct every aspect of my working life with integrity.

I'm always keen to create new relationships with designers and web agencies: if you think we might be able to help each other, do get in touch.
